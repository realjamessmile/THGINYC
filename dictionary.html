DICTIONARY

HORNY (adjective):

horn·y /ˈhôrnē/

1. The state of wanting, or lusting for, something you don’t yet have.
2. To be aroused.

Superlative adjective: horniest. See ‘Tess and Riley’.

AROUSE (transitive verb)

a·rouse /əˈrouz/

1. To awaken from sleep.
2. To excite someone sexually.
3. To provoke.

Present participle: arousing. See 'Tess and Riley'.
